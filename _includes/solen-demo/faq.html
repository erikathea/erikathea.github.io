Buying A Lot or House & Lot

How can I reserve a lot/unit?

Once you have chosen a lot in Solen submit the following documents and payment to GDC's authorized personnel:

  • Reservation Fee: Php 25 000 Lot / Php 50 000 House & Lot
  • Reservation Agreement with manual computation sheet
  • Buyer's Information Sheet
  • Any valid government ID (photocopy, original to be presented)

What are the next steps after reserving a lot/unit?

A notarized reservation agreement and contract to sell will be sent to the buyerâ€™s registered address 1 month after reservation

How can I purchase from abroad?

For buyers based abroad, a notarized or consularized Special Power of Attorney is needed to be submitted to GDC. This is to ensure that the buyer has given his Attorney-in-Fact the authority to be duly representing him
